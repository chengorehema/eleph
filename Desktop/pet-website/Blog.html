h2>Tips for Keeping Your Cat Entertained

Cats are curious and playful creatures, and it's important to keep them mentally and physically stimulated. Here are some fun and easy ways to keep your feline friend entertained:

  • Rotate their toys regularly to keep things interesting.
  • Provide scratching posts and cat trees for climbing and scratching.
  • Play interactive games like chasing a laser pointer or feather wand.
  • Set up a bird feeder outside a window for your cat to watch.
  • Hide treats around the house for them to hunt and find.
